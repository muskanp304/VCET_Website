Undergraduate Programs

4-Year B.E. Programs for Engineering Excellence

💻 B.E. Computer Science & Engineering

Duration: 4 Years | Seats: 120

Comprehensive program covering core computer science disciplines including programming, databases, networks, software engineering, and emerging domains like artificial intelligence, machine learning, and cloud computing.

Key Focus Areas:
  • ✓ Data Structures & Algorithms
  • ✓ Software Development & Testing
  • ✓ Database Management Systems
  • ✓ Artificial Intelligence & ML
  • ✓ Cloud Computing
  • ✓ Cybersecurity

⚙️ B.E. Mechanical Engineering

Duration: 4 Years | Seats: 120

Advanced mechanical engineering program emphasizing thermal systems, manufacturing processes, CAD/CAM technologies, industrial automation, and mechanical design with comprehensive laboratory experience.

Key Focus Areas:
  • ✓ Thermal Engineering
  • ✓ Manufacturing Technology
  • ✓ CAD/CAM & 3D Design
  • ✓ Mechanical Design
  • ✓ Industrial Automation
  • ✓ Robotics & Control

⚡ B.E. Electronics & Telecommunication

Duration: 4 Years | Seats: 60

Specialized program in electronics, communication systems, digital signal processing, and embedded systems with state-of-the-art laboratory facilities and industry partnerships.

Key Focus Areas:
  • ✓ Communication Systems
  • ✓ Digital Signal Processing
  • ✓ Microelectronics
  • ✓ Embedded Systems
  • ✓ VLSI Design
  • ✓ Wireless Technology

🔬 B.E. Instrumentation Engineering

Duration: 4 Years | Seats: 60

Comprehensive program covering process instrumentation, control systems, biomedical engineering, and industrial measurement with hands-on laboratory experience and industry-sponsored projects.

Key Focus Areas:
  • ✓ Process Instrumentation
  • ✓ Control Systems
  • ✓ Biomedical Engineering
  • ✓ Signal Conditioning
  • ✓ Industrial Measurement
  • ✓ Neural Networks
