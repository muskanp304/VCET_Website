Undergraduate Programs

AICTE-approved B.E. programs at VCET.

📈 B.E. Computer Science & Engineering (Data Science)

Duration: 4 Years | Seats: 180

This undergraduate program trains students in data analytics, machine learning, big data technologies, and scientific computing. Students learn to convert raw data into actionable insights through programming, statistics, and modern tools.

Key Focus Areas:
  • ✓ Data Science & Analytics
  • ✓ Machine Learning & AI
  • ✓ Big Data Technologies
  • ✓ Data Visualization
  • ✓ Statistical Modelling

🌐 B.E. Information Technology

Duration: 4 Years | Seats: 60

The IT program emphasizes practical computing, networking, database systems, and software engineering. It is supported by modern labs, high-speed internet, and licensed software to develop industry-ready professionals.

Key Focus Areas:
  • ✓ Software Development
  • ✓ Networking & Security
  • ✓ Web Technologies
  • ✓ Practical IT Labs
  • ✓ Professional Skill Development

🤖 B.E. Artificial Intelligence & Data Science

Duration: 4 Years | Seats: 120

This program develops AI and data science professionals using modern software, research labs, and outcome-based methods. The curriculum includes AI, analytics, computational intelligence, and real-world applications.

Key Focus Areas:
  • ✓ Artificial Intelligence
  • ✓ Data Analytics
  • ✓ Research & Innovation
  • ✓ Outcome-Based Education
  • ✓ IPR and patent development

⚙️ B.E. Mechanical Engineering

Duration: 4 Years | Seats: 60

The Mechanical Engineering department trains students in thermal sciences, manufacturing, design, and renewable energy. It supports hands-on learning through formula cars, quad bikes, and product design projects.

Key Focus Areas:
  • ✓ Design & Manufacturing
  • ✓ Thermal Sciences
  • ✓ Renewable Energy
  • ✓ Industry Internships
  • ✓ Student Engineering Clubs

📡 B.E. Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering

Duration: 4 Years | Seats: 60

The EXTC program offers education in communication systems, embedded systems, DSP, and advanced electronics. It is supported by Texas Instruments labs and active IEEE/IETE student chapters.

Key Focus Areas:
  • ✓ Communication Systems
  • ✓ Embedded Systems
  • ✓ Digital Signal Processing
  • ✓ Industry Projects
  • ✓ Professional Student Chapters

🏗️ B.E. Civil Engineering

Duration: 4 Years | Seats: 60

The Civil Engineering department focuses on technical knowledge, professional skills, and industry collaborations. It encourages students to take part in product showcases and national-level competitions.

Key Focus Areas:
  • ✓ Structural Engineering
  • ✓ Industry Collaboration
  • ✓ Project Showcases
  • ✓ Team & Leadership Skills
