About VCET

Our Heritage, Values, and Commitment to Excellence

Our Heritage

Vidyavardhini's College of Engineering & Technology was established in 1970 by the late Padmashri H. G. alias Bhausaheb Vartak. The college was founded on the noble principle of providing quality education in rural areas, embodying the belief that education is the foundation of societal progress.

Over five decades, VCET has evolved from a vision into a reality of academic excellence. Vidyavardhini means "a body committed to the advancement of knowledge", and this principle continues to guide every aspect of our operations.

Today, VCET stands as one of Maharashtra's premier autonomous engineering institutions, recognized for its commitment to holistic development of students and groundbreaking research initiatives.

Our Mission

"To provide a technologically inspiring environment for learning that fosters innovation, research, and excellence in engineering education."

Our Vision

"To be a center of excellence in engineering education and research, contributing to the nation's technological advancement and societal well-being."
