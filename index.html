About VCET

Vidyavardhini's College of Engineering & Technology is a premier autonomous engineering institution committed to providing a technologically inspiring environment for learning and innovation.

Founded in 1970 by late Padmashri H. G. alias Bhausaheb Vartak, VCET was established for the noble cause of education in rural areas. Over the decades, we have evolved into one of the leading engineering colleges in Maharashtra.

Our Mission: To provide technologically inspiring environment for learning and to foster innovation, research, and excellence in engineering education.
