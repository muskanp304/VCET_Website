About VCET

Vidyavardhini's College of Engineering & Technology is a premier autonomous engineering institution committed to providing a technologically inspiring environment for learning and innovation.

Founded in 1970 by late Padmashri H. G. alias Bhausaheb Vartak, VCET was established for the noble cause of education in rural areas. Over the decades, we have evolved into one of the leading engineering colleges in Maharashtra.

Our Mission: To provide technologically inspiring environment for learning and to foster innovation, research, and excellence in engineering education.

Academic Programs

VCET offers AICTE-approved undergraduate courses that match the college’s current intake and curriculum.

B.E. Computer Science & Engineering (Data Science)

Duration: 4 Years | Seats: 180

This flagship course trains students in data analytics, machine learning, artificial intelligence, and modern computing tools used by industry today.

  • Data Science & Analytics
  • Machine Learning, AI and Deep Learning
  • Big Data Technologies and Visualization
  • Industry-oriented projects and research labs

B.E. Information Technology

Duration: 4 Years | Seats: 60

This program focuses on software development, network engineering, database management, and practical IT systems for modern enterprises.

  • Software engineering and application development
  • Networking, cybersecurity and cloud-ready skills
  • Database systems and web technologies
  • Practical labs, internships and industry exposure

B.E. Artificial Intelligence & Data Science

Duration: 4 Years | Seats: 120

A cutting-edge program centered on AI, analytics, smart systems, and research-based learning to prepare students for the digital future.

  • AI and Deep Learning techniques
  • Data-driven decision making and predictive modelling
  • Outcome-based learning and innovation projects
  • Industry mentorship and IPR awareness

B.E. Mechanical Engineering

Duration: 4 Years | Seats: 60

The mechanical program provides hands-on training in design, manufacturing, thermal engineering, and renewable energy systems.

  • Design and CAD/CAM engineering
  • Thermal systems and energy management
  • Formula car, quad bike and robotics projects
  • Industry internships and technical competitions

B.E. Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering

Duration: 4 Years | Seats: 60

This course equips students in communication systems, digital signal processing, embedded systems, and electronics lab practice.

  • Communications and RF engineering
  • Embedded systems and IoT applications
  • Signal processing and microelectronics
  • Industry collaboration with TI labs and IEEE chapters

B.E. Civil Engineering

Duration: 4 Years | Seats: 60

The civil engineering program focuses on structural design, construction technology, surveying, and sustainable infrastructure development.

  • Structural engineering fundamentals
  • Construction materials and project management
  • Surveying, hydraulics, and geotechnical labs
  • Industry-focused sessions and practical training
